# Campus & Facilities

## Library

- Housed in a four-storey building known as the **Ruby Block** (Source: /library)
- Collection: textbooks, reference books, novels, encyclopaedias, magazines, and national/international journals; also co-curricular and competitive-exam resource materials (Source: /library)
- Book count, exact title count, and seating capacity: the library page displays "VOLUMES," "TITLES," and "SEATING CAPACITY" as headline statistics, but these are rendered as dynamic counters that did not resolve to numeric values in the page content retrieved — **exact figures are Not published on website** in the static content reviewed (Source: /library)
- Similarly, "NATIONAL JOURNALS," "MAGAZINES," "ELECTRONIC JOURNALS," and "CD" counts are displayed as dynamic counters without resolved numeric values in the content reviewed (Source: /library)
- Digital library: 10 computers with internet facility, providing access to subscribed online content, NPTEL video lectures, and online mock tests (Source: /library)
- Digital/e-resources: member of **DELNET** and the **National Digital Library of India (NDLI)**; e-journal packages **IEEE-ASPP** and **J-GATE (JET)**, accessible via the KNIMBUS mLibrary platform and mobile app; remote access provided via the Knimbus e-Library platform with self-registration using institute email (Source: /library)
- Cataloguing system: **Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC)**; library is automated using the **Engineering College Automation Package (ECAP)**, with OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) access (Source: /library)
- Plagiarism check: the library administers **iThenticate** (a Turnitin product) for staff and student publications; final-year UG/PG projects are checked when submitted through the project guide/coordinator (Source: /library)
- Rare books: the library states it holds a rare-book collection with items printed from 1919 onwards (Source: /library)
- Library hours: **9:00 AM – 6:00 PM**; Issue & Return window: **10:00 AM – 5:00 PM** (Source: /library)
- Floor-wise directory:
  - Ground floor: Librarian's Desk, Digital Library, Periodicals Section, Newspaper Section, Back Volumes, Reading Area
  - First floor: Circulation Counter I, Stack Area (CSE, IT & BS&H), Reading Area
  - Second floor: Circulation Counter II, Stack Area (ECE & EEE), Reading Area
  - Third floor: Reference Section, Project Reports, Reading Area, Activity Area
  (Source: /library)
- Book Bank: the library maintains a Book Bank for SC/ST students, providing additional books via the Social Welfare Department, Government of Telangana (Source: /library)
- Literary club: the library supports a literary club named **"PORTIA: The Literary Coven"** (Source: /library)

## Hostel

- Hostel buildings are located within the college campus (Source: /admission/hostel)
- ⚠️ Conflicting figures found on the same page — the narrative body text states: "We have 4 blocks of hostels with **150+ rooms** and occupancy of **500+**" (Source: /admission/hostel), while the page's separate "Highlights" bullet list states "**300+ Occupancy**" and "**500+ Rooms**" (Source: /admission/hostel). The room-count and occupancy figures are transposed/inconsistent between the two sections of the same page — verify before use.
- The page does not distinguish separate boys'/girls' hostel details (BVRITH is a women's college, so hostel facilities described are for women students) (Source: /admission/hostel)
- Staffing/safety: experienced lady wardens and security officers are stated to be allotted to each block; a full-time lady physical director conducts outdoor games (volleyball, badminton, throw-ball, etc.); the hostel is described as "hundred percent ragging free" with an anti-ragging squad (Source: /admission/hostel)
- Amenities highlighted: 24/7 water supply, 24/7 security, geyser facility, a "fresh choice bakery," a fast-food center, a gym, yoga & sports facilities, and "eco-friendly surroundings" (Source: /admission/hostel)
- A common room and television system (for news/sports/entertainment) are provided (Source: /admission/hostel)
- Room types (AC/non-AC, sharing basis) and specific hostel fee amounts: Not published on website in the pages reviewed (see Fee Structure section)
- Curfew/detailed rules: Not published on website in the pages reviewed
