# About BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

## Institution identity

- Full institution name: **BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women (BVRITH)** (Source: /, /about-bvrith)
- Founding year: **2012** (Source: /, /about-bvrith)
- Parent body: BVRITH is described as "the youngest offspring of Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES)," founded by Chairman Sri K V Vishnu Raju (Source: /about-bvrith)
- Affiliating university: **Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH)** (Source: /about-bvrith)
- Institution type: **Autonomous**, private engineering college for women. The college was granted UGC autonomous status from AY 2023–24 for a period of 10 years (Source: /about-bvrith)

## Vision statement

"To emerge as the best among the institutes of technology and research in the country dedicated to the cause of promoting quality technical education." (Source: /about-bvrith)

## Mission statement

At BVRITH, we strive to:
- Achieve academic excellence through innovative learning practices.
- Enhance intellectual ability and technical competency for a successful career.
- Encourage research and innovation.
- Nurture students towards holistic development with emphasis on leadership skills, life skills and human values.
(Source: /about-bvrith)

- **NAAC**: Accredited with Grade 'A' (CGPA 3.23) in the year 2020 (Source: /about-bvrith)
- **NBA**: The About page states "All 4 branches (CSE, ECE, IT & EEE) are NBA accredited in 2018" (Source: /about-bvrith). ⚠️ Conflicting figures found — the site-wide announcement banner instead states "ACCREDITED BY NBA FOR EEE, ECE, CSE" (i.e., it does not list IT) (Source: /). A dated NBA approval document on the site is titled "NBA-2025-28," implying the accreditation period has since been renewed/extended, but the specific branches and validity dates covered by that renewal are not stated in the page text reviewed — verify before use.
- **AICTE**: The Institute is stated to be "duly approved by All India Council for Technical Education, Government of Telangana" (Source: /about-bvrith)
- **UGC**: Autonomous status granted from AY 2023–24 for 10 years (Source: /about-bvrith)
- The site also links approval/accreditation documents for AICTE, NBA, NAAC (AQAR cycles for 2019-20, 2020-21, 2021-22, 2022-23), ARIIA, AISHE, and Unnath Bharat Abhiyan, but the detailed contents of these linked PDFs were not reviewed for this document (Source: / — Approvals menu)

## Notable achievements (as stated on the About page)

- Entered the Asia Book of Records for an attempt at the "largest Mental Health Peer Counseling for Women" on 7 March 2018 (Source: /about-bvrith)
- States it was the first college to initiate mentorship programs with MNCs such as Microsoft, Amazon, and Qualcomm (Source: /about-bvrith)
- Received two JNTUH University Gold Medals in two consecutive years, out of three batches graduated at the time of writing (Source: /about-bvrith)

## Programs offered (summary — see Departments section for details)

- Ph.D research centres in ECE (VLSI-Communications) and CSE (Source: /)
