# Fee Structure

⚠️ Limited information found for this section. The website publishes tuition fee tables by admitted batch/year (below), but hostel fees, mess/food charges, transport fees, one-time charges (admission fee, caution deposit), itemized exam/library/lab fees, scholarship details, payment schedule, and refund policy were not located on the pages reviewed for this document.

All figures below are in Indian Rupees (₹) per year and are broken into three components as published: Tuition Fee, NBA Fee, and JNTUH/Misc. Fee. Figures are reproduced exactly as tabulated on the site, batch by batch — they are **not** averaged or combined across years.

## Fee category variants (Convener vs. Management quota)

The site distinguishes "Category A" (Convener/TS EAMCET-filled) and "Category B" (college-filled, per TSCHE guidelines) admission routes (Source: /admission/admission-process), but a separate fee figure specific to Category B/management-quota seats was not located on the pages reviewed. Whether the tuition figures above apply to Category A only, or to both categories, is not stated explicitly on the Fee Details page and should be verified directly with the college.
