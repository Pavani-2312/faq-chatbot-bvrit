# Admissions

## Eligibility criteria

- Minimum qualifying background: 10+2 with MPC (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry) as major subjects (Source: /admission/admission-process)
- Minimum marks: **60%** (Source: /admission/admission-process)
- Selection also governed by TS EAMCET selection criteria (Source: /admission/admission-process)

## Entrance exams / admission routes accepted

- **Category A seats**: filled by the Convener, TS EAMCET (TG EAPCET) Admissions (Source: /admission/admission-process)
- **Category B seats**: filled by the college directly, as per TSCHE (Telangana State Council of Higher Education) guidelines (Source: /admission/admission-process)
- College codes stated on the website: **EAMCET/ECET code: BVRW**; **PGECET code: BVRW1** (Source: /)
- A "B-Category" admissions page exists on the site (for management-quota-style Category B seats) but its specific eligibility/fee rules were not reviewed for this document (Source: /admission/b-category)
- Lateral entry (ECET) rules: Not published on website in the pages reviewed, though "ECET" is referenced in the site's admissions code banner (Source: /) — specific lateral-entry policy details were not located.

## Application process (as described on the Admission Process page)

1. After TS EAMCET releases its notification (for Category A / convener seats) or TSCHE releases its notification (for Category B seats), applicants may apply.
2. Category A applicants apply online through the Convener–TS EAMCET portal.
3. Category B applicants apply online through the college website when the TSCHE notification/application form becomes available under the Admissions tab.
4. No re-application is described as necessary.
5. The complete counseling/admission schedule is stated to be published by TSCHE.
(Source: /admission/admission-process)
