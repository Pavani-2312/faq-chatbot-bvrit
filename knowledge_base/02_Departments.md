## Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

- Branch name & short code: Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department)
- Year established: The B.Tech. CSE program started in the academic year 2012–13 (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department)
- Intake capacity: Started with an intake of 60 in 2012–13; increased to 120 from AY 2014–15; increased to 180 from AY 2019–20; increased to 360 from AY 2024–25 (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department). The current intake table (2025–26 admissions) separately lists CSE intake as **360** (Source: /admission/intake-of-courses)
- Faculty count: **49 faculty members** — 07 Professors, 06 Associate Professors, 36 Assistant Professors, plus adjunct faculty from industry and academia (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department)
- HOD name: Not published on website (an "About HOD" subpage exists at /computer-science-and-engineering/about-hod/ but was not reviewed for this document)
- Department-specific accreditation: The B.Tech. CSE program is stated to be "accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for a period of Three years from the academic year 2018–2019 and extended till the academic year 2021–2022" (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department). ⚠️ This period predates the current date; whether/how the NBA accreditation has since been renewed for CSE specifically is not confirmed in the pages reviewed (see the NBA conflict note in the About section).
- Specializations/electives (example courses cited): Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Cloud Computing, Software Engineering, Data Analytics, Cyber Security, Information Theory & Coding, Advanced Computer Architecture, Natural Language Processing (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department)
- Department-specific labs: Not published on website in the page reviewed (a dedicated "Laboratories" subpage exists at /computer-science-and-engineering/laboratories/ but was not reviewed for this document)
- Vision: "Develop women as technocrats, researchers and entrepreneurs in the field of computer science and engineering." (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department)
- Mission: To impart quality education in Computer Science and Engineering by means of learning techniques and value-added courses; to inculcate professional excellence and research culture by encouraging projects in cutting-edge technologies through industry interactions; to build leadership skills, ethical values and teamwork among students; to strengthen the collaboration of department and industry through internships, mentorships and professional body activities (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department)
- Student clubs/technical activities named for CSE: ACM Student Chapter, CSI Student Branch, department technical association "TechBlitz," and coding platforms e-Lab, HackersBay, CodeChef, HackerRank (Source: /computer-science-and-engineering/about-the-department)
