## Placement percentage

An overall placement **percentage** (i.e., placed students ÷ eligible students × 100) for any batch is **not published on website** in the pages reviewed. Only absolute headcounts of placed students per company/branch/batch are published (see tables above).

## Average / median package

Not published on website in the pages reviewed. Only per-company package figures (used to derive the "highest package" per batch above) are published; a computed batch-wide average or median CTC was not located.

## Internship program details

Not published on website in the pages reviewed. (An "Internships" page exists at /placements/internships/ but was not reviewed for this document.)

## Branch-wise placement statistics

Branch-wise headcounts (EEE/ECE/CSE/IT/CSE-AI&ML) are published per company per batch — see the tables above. A separately computed branch-wise placement *percentage* is not published on website in the pages reviewed.
