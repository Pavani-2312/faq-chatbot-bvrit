## About ISTE

The **Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE)** is the leading national professional non-profit society for the technical education system in India, with the motto of career development of teachers and personality development of students.

## ISTE Branch at BVRIT HYDERABAD

The BVRIT HYDERABAD ISTE Student Branch was inaugurated in **2013** with:

- **Branch No:** AP-175
- **Institute Membership No:** IM2278

Under ISTE, all departments conduct Paper Presentations, Poster Presentations, Technical Quiz, Guest Lectures, and Workshops to help students become top-quality professional engineers needed by industry.
