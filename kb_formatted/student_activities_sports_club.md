## Overview

BVRIT Hyderabad provides sports and games to support student physical fitness, mental well-being and overall development. The college records participation and achievements across multiple inter-college, state-level and university-level tournaments.

## Notable Participation

- BVRITH champions participated in the 2015 South Zone Shuttle Badminton event.
- BVRITH champions participated in the 2016 All India Shuttle Badminton event.
