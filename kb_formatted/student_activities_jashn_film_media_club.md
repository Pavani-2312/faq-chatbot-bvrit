## About JASHN

**JASHN** is the official Film and Media Club of BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women, guided by the motto: *"Where Requirements Meet Talent."* It brings together creative minds across all domains of cinema and media — dancers, writers, actors, singers, videographers, photographers, and editors — to collaborate and transform ideas into impactful visual stories.

## Objectives

- **Creative Integration:** Bring together diverse talents for filmmaking and media production
- **Skill Enhancement:** Develop skills in acting, direction, scriptwriting, dance, music, cinematography, photography, editing, and digital storytelling
- **Talent Utilisation:** Meet creative requirements for college events, festivals, and promotions using in-house student talent
- **Cultural Growth:** Promote appreciation for cinema, arts, and media through events and screenings
- **Digital Media Presence:** Strengthen the college's social media and digital outreach through visual and reel-based content

## Activities Conducted

- **Chitraanga** — Inter-College Short Film Competition encouraging creativity and technical innovation
- **Festive and Cultural Celebrations** — Performances and media coverage for New Year, Christmas, and college festival events
- **Movie Screenings** — Sessions to promote cinematic appreciation, analysis, and discussion
- **Content Creation & Social Media Management** — Reels, posters, promotional videos for college activities
- **Media Contributions** — Dancers, singers, actors, photographers, videographers, and editors for college programs
