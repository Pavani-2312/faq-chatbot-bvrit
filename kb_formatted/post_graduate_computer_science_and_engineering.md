## Programme Overview

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at BVRITH started in 2012. The M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering started in 2024 with an intake of 12.

The department has state-of-the-art infrastructure and computing equipment supported by high-speed internet and wireless networks. It provides a research environment supported by teaching, industry-institute collaboration, sponsored research projects and consultancy services.
