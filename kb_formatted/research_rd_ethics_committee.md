## About the REC

The **Research Ethics Committee (REC)** at BVRIT HYDERABAD ensures that research across all departments is conducted with integrity and in adherence to ethical standards. The REC also functions as the **Research Outcome Evaluation Committee (ROEC)**, extending its role to the overall promotion and evaluation of research quality.

## Responsibilities — REC

- Review research proposals and protocols
- Conduct ethics assessments and grant approvals
- Monitor ongoing research and perform periodic audits
- Offer guidance and training on ethical research practices
- Investigate and address allegations of unethical conduct

## Responsibilities — ROEC

- Encourage faculty and students to engage in high-quality research
- Create awareness about funding opportunities from government, industry, and international agencies
- Motivate faculty for quality publications and collaborative research
- Maintain records on publications, patents, funded projects, and consultancy work
- Organise seminars, FDPs, workshops, hackathons, and conferences on research and innovation
