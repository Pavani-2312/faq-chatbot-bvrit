**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/temple/
**Category:** Campus Facilities

---

## Overview

BVRITH has a temple on campus dedicated to **Lord Hanuman**, who is the presiding deity of BVRITH and is self-manifested, blessing all the BVRITH fraternity.

## Daily Schedule

- Daily pooja is offered by the priest every day
- Special Aarathi is performed on **Tuesday** and **Saturday**

## Highlights

- Blessed stone
- Hanuman temple
- Special pooja days (Tuesday & Saturday)
