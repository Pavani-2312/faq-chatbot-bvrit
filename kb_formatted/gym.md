# Gym — Fitness Centre

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/gym/
**Category:** Campus Facilities

---

## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD has a well-equipped Fitness Centre (Gym) on campus. Students can make use of the gym facilities during their comfortable time under the guidance of an experienced instructor.

A state of complete physical, mental and social well-being is essential for students — the college recognizes this and provides dedicated gym facilities to support student health and fitness.

## Key Features

- Well-equipped gym with modern fitness equipment
- Available to all students
- Guided by experienced instructor
- Supports physical fitness alongside academic excellence
