## About ACM

The **Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)** is the world's largest computing society, bringing together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges.

**ACM Chapters** are local nodes of activity offering seminars, lectures, and networking opportunities. **ACM-W Student Chapters** specifically support women in computing, providing mentoring, bonding over common interests, and community outreach both within and outside institutions.

## ACM-W Student Branch at BVRIT HYDERABAD

The **BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women ACM-W Student Branch** was established in **2019**. It regularly organises:

- Workshops and seminars
- Distinguished and Eminent Speaker Programs
- Hour of Code
- Annual events and Hackathons
- Engineer's Day and Innovation Day celebrations
- Coding contests and Design Thinking events
- Weekly skill-development events

## Benefits of ACM-W Membership

- Unlimited access to ACM's collection of thousands of online books, courses, and training videos
- Full access to the ACM Digital Library with articles from all ACM publications
- Access to ACM's online magazine for practising software engineers
- Discounts on subscriptions to ACM journals, magazines, books, and conferences
- Special discounts through ACM's Insurance, Discounts, and Special Offers Programs

## Institutional Membership

BVRIT HYDERABAD holds an ACM Institutional Membership, enabling all students to benefit from ACM resources.
