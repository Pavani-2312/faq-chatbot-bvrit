## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD Toastmasters Club was chartered on **19 June 2017** with 32 members and has conducted **230+ meetings** so far. It provides a supportive and positive learning experience where members develop communication, leadership skills, and self-confidence.

> "Life is not a problem to be solved, but a reality to be experienced." — Soren Kierkegaard

## Meeting Schedule

- **Frequency:** Every week on Saturday, 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM
- **Format:**
  1. Prepared speeches (11 educational modules)
  2. Impromptus speeches
  3. Evaluation
