## About Rotaract Club

The **Rotaract Club of BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women** is a social service club for young people aged 18 and older. It is part of **Rotary Club International**, a global network of 1.4 million people with 46,000+ clubs worldwide.

## Activities

The club:
- Exchanges ideas with community leaders
- Develops leadership and professional skills among students
- Conducts community service activities
- Fosters fun through meaningful service projects
