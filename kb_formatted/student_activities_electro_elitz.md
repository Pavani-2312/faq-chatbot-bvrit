## Overview

Electro Elitz is the Technical Association of the ECE Department. It was inaugurated on 24 September 2013 by Dr. K. V. N. Sunitha, Principal, BVRIT Hyderabad, and Prof. N. Mangala Gouri, ECE, JNTUH. Students of II, III and IV ECE are members of the association.
