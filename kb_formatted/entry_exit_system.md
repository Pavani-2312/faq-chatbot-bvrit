**Category:** Campus Facilities / Academic Regulations

## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women (UGC Autonomous) adopts an **Entry–Exit System** as per **NEP 2020**.

The Entry–Exit system allows students flexibility in their academic journey. For full details, refer to **Section 12.9** of the Academic Regulations (BH23).

## Reference

- Academic Regulations (BH23) — Section 12.9 covers the Entry–Exit policy
- Applicable under UGC Autonomous status granted from AY 2023-24
