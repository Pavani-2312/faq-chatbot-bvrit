# Plagiarism Tool

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/research/plagiarism-tool/
**Category:** Research

---

## Overview

iThenticate is a plagiarism detection tool used by researchers, academic institutions and publishers to check the originality of written work. It was developed by Turnitin and is designed for professional research and scholarly content.

## Use Cases

iThenticate is used to check:

- Manuscripts
- Research papers
- Grant proposals
- Academic publications before submission

## Benefits

- **Pre-submission checks:** Researchers can verify originality before submitting work to journals.
- **Efficiency:** The tool automates plagiarism checking for large documents.
- **Credibility:** It helps ensure research work is free from plagiarism and supports ethical publication standards.

## Academic Integrity

iThenticate supports academic integrity by helping ensure that published research is original and properly cited.
