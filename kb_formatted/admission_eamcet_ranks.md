## About EAMCET Ranks

BVRIT HYDERABAD publishes branch-wise EAMCET closing rank data to help students assess their admission chances. The rank analysis covers all branches offered at the college.

- **College EAMCET/ECET Code:** BVRW
- **PGECET Code:** BVRW1

To check eligibility, compare your TS EAMCET rank with the closing ranks from the previous year.
