# Admission Process

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/admission-process/
**Category:** Admissions

---

## How to Apply

- **Category A Seats:** Filled by the Convener, TSEAMCET Admissions
- **Category B Seats:** Filled by the College as per the guidelines of TSCHE

## When to Apply

After the notification released by TSEAMCET (for convener seats) and TSCHE (for B-category seats).

## Application Mode

- Category A: Apply online through the Convener–TSEAMCET portal
- Category B: Apply online through the college website when the TSCHE notification is released (form available under the Admissions tab)

## Eligibility / Prerequisites

- 10+2 with MPC (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry) as major subjects
- Minimum 60% marks
- Must qualify TS EAMCET (TG EAPCET)

## FAQ

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Can I apply online? | Yes — Category A via TSEAMCET portal; Category B via college website |
| Where to post the form? | Form available in Admissions tab after TSCHE notification |
| How long to wait for reply? | Complete schedule released by TSCHE |
| Can I re-apply? | Not required |
| How to check admission chances? | Check last year EAMCET category-wise rank details on the college website |

## Reference

- Rank Analysis of TG EAPCET 2025-26 available on the college website
- TS EAMCET 2023-24 ranks available for reference under Admissions tab
