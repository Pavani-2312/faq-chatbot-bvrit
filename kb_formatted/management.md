## Late Dr. B. V. Raju — Founder Chairman

Late **Padma Bhushan Dr. B. V. Raju** was the founder chairman of Dr. B.V. Raju Foundation and Dr. B.V. Raju Institute and Technologies.

- Started his career as a low-grade employee in the cement industry; rose to become Managing Director and Chairman of Cement Corporation of India
- Established multiple factories under the **Raasi Group of Industries**: Sri Vishnu Cements Ltd., Raasi Refractories Ltd., Raasi Ceramics Ltd., and Raasi Finance and Investments Ltd.
- Awarded the **Padma Bhushan** by the Government of India
- Awarded **Honorary Doctorate** by JNTU Hyderabad
- Established colleges under Dr. B.V. Raju Foundation and Sri Vishnu Educational Society:
  - **BVRIT (Narsapur)** — Dr. B.V. Raju Institute of Technology, Medak District
  - **Bhimavaram Campus** — includes Smt. B. Seetha Polytechnic, Shri Vishnu College of Pharmacy, Sri Vishnu Engineering College for Women, Vishnu Dental College, Vishnu Public School

## Sri K. V. Vishnu Raju — Chairman

**Sri K. V. Vishnu Raju** took over as Chairman of Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES) after the passing of Founder Chairman Dr. B.V. Raju in 2002.

- Alumnus of REC Trichi and **Michigan Technological University (USA)**
- Former Chemical Engineer at **DuPont DE Nemours, Wilmington, Delaware, USA**
- Industrialist, Educationist, and Social Entrepreneur
- Steered SVES into a pioneer of internationally acceptable engineering, medical, and professional education

## Sri Aditya Vissam — Secretary

**Sri Aditya Vissam** is the Secretary of Sri Vishnu Educational Society and the youngest member of the management team.

- Industrial Engineering graduate — **Penn State University, USA**
- MBA — **Imperial College, London**
- Former Operations Manager at **Amazon, London**
- Former employee at **Office Depot, Minneapolis**
