# Internships

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/placements/internships/
**Category:** Placements

---

## Overview

Students at BVRITH get internship opportunities right from their **1st year through to the final year**.

## Key Highlights

- Stipends as high as **₹1.1 lakh/month** have been offered during internships
- Duration is generally **2–6 months**
- Internships come through campus placements or off-campus with college guidance
- Many internship opportunities are converted to **full-time job offers**

## Companies Offering Internships (Sample)

- Microsoft
- Amazon
- Qualcomm
- Flipkart
- Target
- Qlik
- Many other opportunities available
