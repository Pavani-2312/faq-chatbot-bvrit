## Overview

The B.Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) at BVRIT HYDERABAD has a total intake of 180 seats (expanded to 360 seats). The department aims to be an important regional, national, and international resource centre for the development of Computing, IT and Software systems. Over 75% of students are placed in multinational companies such as Capgemini, IBM, Adobe, Mu Sigma, Bank of America, State Street, Open Text, TCS, Tech Mahindra, and many more.

## Course Highlights

- **Degree:** B.Tech. (4-year program offered by BVRIT-H)
- Ability to design hardware, software systems, components and processes to meet desired needs within realistic constraints
- Trains students to function on multi-disciplinary teams, formulate and solve engineering problems, and design algorithms and protocols
- Career domains: Software Development (Front-end, Back-end), Network Design, Network Management, Data Analytics, Software Testing, System Software Development
