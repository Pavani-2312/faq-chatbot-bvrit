## Overview

The Department of **Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)** was established in **2012** with an intake of 60, increased to 120 from AY 2014-15. The department initiated a **PG Program in VLSI Design** from AY 2024-25 and a **Ph.D Programme (VLSI-Communications)** from AY 2026-27.

**NBA Accreditation:** First accreditation in AY 2018–19; latest accreditation granted in 2025–26 for another three-year period.

## Programs Offered

- **B.Tech** in Electronics and Communication Engineering — intake: 120
- **M.Tech** in VLSI Design — intake: 12
- **Ph.D** (Centre: VLSI – Communications)

## Vision

> To emerge as a center of academic and research excellence in Electronics and Communication Engineering and create globally competent women technocrats with a high degree of social consciousness.

## Mission

- **M1:** Provide quality education with emphasis on training in latest technologies as per industrial needs
- **M2:** Impart research culture, professional ethics and moral values
- **M3:** Inculcate in students the ability to identify real-life problems and evolve effective solutions

## Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

- **PEO-1:** Excel in contemporary problem solving in ECE through knowledge-base provided by the program
- **PEO-2:** Demonstrate technical, communication and leadership skills professionally or as entrepreneurs with social responsibility
- **PEO-3:** Analyze latest technology growth in ECE and update knowledge continuously
- **PEO-4:** Demonstrate ethical and human values in multicultural environments

## Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

- **PSO-1:** Provide optimized solutions for digital, signal processing and communication systems
- **PSO-2:** Develop compact, energy-efficient and low-cost products to assist differently-abled people
