## About DEEPTHA

**DEEPTHA** is the Technical Association of the EEE Department at BVRIT HYDERABAD, established on **20 March 2014**. It encourages students to showcase their extra-curricular interests and exhibit all-round performance, improving presentation skills, organisational skills, and leadership qualities.

DEEPTHA conducts Poster Presentations, Workshops, and Technical events such as Extempore, Debate, Product Launch, Group Discussions, Circuit Debugging, and more.
