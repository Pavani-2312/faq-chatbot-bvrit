## About the IPR Committee

The **Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Committee** oversees and manages matters related to intellectual property within BVRIT HYDERABAD. Its primary goal is to promote innovation while safeguarding the rights of creators and inventors.

## Responsibilities

- **Policy Development:** Create and update policies related to IP management and protection
- **Review and Assessment:** Evaluate inventions, research outcomes, and creative works for potential IP protection
- **Support and Guidance:** Help researchers and creators navigate the process of securing intellectual property rights
