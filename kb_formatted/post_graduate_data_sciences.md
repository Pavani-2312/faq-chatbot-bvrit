# Data Sciences

**Category:** Postgraduate Programs

---

## Programme Overview

The two-year M.Tech in Data Sciences is offered under the Department of Information Technology. The course covers the subject through interactive lectures, hands-on lab sessions and projects.

## Curriculum Structure

As per JNTUH regulation, the first year includes three core subjects, two elective subjects, two lab subjects and one audit course per semester. In the third semester, students take one core elective, one open elective and Phase 1 of project work. The final semester is dedicated to project work.

## Subject Coverage

The curriculum covers statistics, key programming languages, machine learning algorithms and related data science topics, with a major project.
