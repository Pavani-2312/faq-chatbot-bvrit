## About GSAC

The **Graduate Study Abroad Center (GSAC)** was formed to support SVES students who aspire to pursue higher studies abroad. It guides students and parents in finding the right destination, university, and course. A centralised GSAC Cell operates from the SVES Head Office with SPOCs at each SVES institution.

## Services Offered

- **Counselling:** GSAC SPOCs guide students on courses and locations based on their academic profile
- **Student Loans:** Help obtaining education loans through associated banks
- **Scholarships:** Details on scholarship availability and support in applying
- **Pre-Departure Grooming Programs:** Orientation on culture, money management, safety, and security at the destination

## Destinations Supported

USA · UK · Australia · Canada · France · Germany · Italy
