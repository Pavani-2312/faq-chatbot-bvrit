## About Seed Funding

Seed funding is an in-house financial support programme for faculty research at BVRIT HYDERABAD. It covers consumables, mini equipment, characterisation fees, software purchases, and other operational expenses — enabling faculty to develop stronger proposals for external funding.

## Objectives

- Support pilot research projects, helping faculty secure external grants and industry partnerships
- Explore novel research ideas and generate preliminary data to strengthen external funding proposals
- Foster collaboration among faculty from different departments in emerging research fields
