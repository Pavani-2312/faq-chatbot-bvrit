## About RAKSHANA

**RAKSHANA — Your Shield** is the Women's Safety Cell at BVRIT HYDERABAD, dedicated to ensuring the safety, security, and well-being of all female students, faculty, and staff. It proactively addresses harassment, abuse, and cybercrimes through workshops, counseling, a 24/7 helpline, and collaborations with the SHE Team.

## Objectives

- **Safety and Security:** Establish a safe and secure environment for women on campus
- **Support and Counseling:** Provide confidential support for individuals facing harassment or discrimination
- **Awareness and Education:** Educate the campus community about women's safety, gender equality, and cybercrime prevention
- **Grievance Redressal:** Address and resolve complaints related to harassment, abuse, and cybercrimes effectively

## Activities

- **Workshops and Seminars:** Regular workshops on self-defense, legal rights, gender sensitisation, and cyber safety
- **Counseling Services:** Confidential counseling support
- **24/7 Helpline:** Immediate assistance and reporting of incidents
- **Safety Audits:** Regular audits to identify and mitigate campus risks
- **Awareness Campaigns:** Campaigns on women's rights, safety, and cyber safety
- **SHE Team Collaboration:** Partnership with the SHE Team for enhanced safety measures and joint awareness initiatives
