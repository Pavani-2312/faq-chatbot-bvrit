## Start-Up 2: Srija Green Galaxy — Biopots for Nature

- **Founded by:** Ms. A. Srija (24WH1A0592)
- **UDYAM Registration:** UDYAM-TS-15-0000456
- **Founded:** 2022

**About:** An innovative startup converting agro-waste (groundnut shells) into eco-friendly, biodegradable plant pots. The biopots decompose naturally into nutrient-rich compost, promoting sustainable farming and reducing plastic waste.

**Key achievements:**
- 20,000+ units sold
- 3 regular institutional customers
- Field trials conducted with horticultural departments
- Seeking funding for advanced machinery to scale production and diversify into biodegradable plates and Vinayaka idols
