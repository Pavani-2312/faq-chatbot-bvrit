## About the Foreign Language Programme

In the era of globalisation, BVRIT HYDERABAD offers foreign language courses to equip students with linguistic proficiency, intercultural competence, and enhanced career prospects — both in India and abroad.

## German Language

- Running since **2013** in collaboration with the **Vivekananda Institute**
- Conducted on campus alongside the regular curriculum
- **Level 1 certification** course
- **Six batches** have successfully completed the course

## French Language

- Started in **2018** in collaboration with **Alliance Française, Hyderabad**
- **Two batches** have successfully completed the course

## Benefits

- Linguistic proficiency and intercultural harmony
- Better career prospects in multinational companies
- Support for studying abroad, emigration, and travel
- Increased opportunities in French and German-speaking MNCs
