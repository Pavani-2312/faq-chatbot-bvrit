## About ATL

The **Assistive Technology Lab (ATL)** at BVRIT HYDERABAD was set up in **2013** under the vision of Shri K. V. Vishnu Raju, Chairman of Sri Vishnu Educational Society. It focuses on designing projects that benefit differently-abled persons, mainstreaming people with disabilities into society.

**International Collaborator:** Prof. Alan Rux, Founder of the Assistive Technology Program at the University of Massachusetts Lowell, USA, conducts regular video-conference classes and guides student project teams.
