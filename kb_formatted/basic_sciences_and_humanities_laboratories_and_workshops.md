# Basic Sciences and Humanities — Laboratories and Workshops

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/basic-sciences-and-humanities/laboratories-and-workshops/
**Category:** Departments — BS&H

---

## Overview

The BS&H Department is equipped with the following labs and workshops, serving all first-year engineering students:

| Lab | Count |
|-----|-------|
| English Language and Communication Skills Lab | 2 |
| Applied Physics Lab | 2 |
| Engineering Chemistry Lab | 2 |
| Engineering Workshop | 2 |
| Programming for Problem Solving Lab | 1 |
| Basic Electrical Engineering Lab | 1 |
| Advanced Communication Skills Lab | 1 |

---

## English Language and Communication Skills Lab

Equipped with surround sound audio setup, LCD projectors, headphones, collar microphones, English licensed software, and computers with updated configurations. Covers:
- Listening and Speaking skills
- Reading, Writing, Grammar and Vocabulary
- Stress, pause, intonation and grammar patterns
- Preparation for job interviews and placements

---

## Applied Physics Lab

Designed to develop experimental skills in:
- Semiconductors
- Optoelectronics
- Lasers
- Fibre Optics
- Electromagnetism

---

## Engineering Chemistry Lab

Hands-on experience in:
- Chemical analysis and characterization
- Sample preparation and analysis of liquid and solid samples
- State-of-the-art equipment for complex experiments
- Two spacious, well-ventilated labs with dedicated safety protocols

---

## Engineering Workshop

Imparts practical knowledge in:
- Fitting
- Tin smithy
- Carpentry
- Basic manufacturing techniques

Provides a bird's eye view of practical activities in various sections of a manufacturing unit.

---

## Programming for Problem Solving Lab

C programming fundamentals: control structures, arrays, functions, pointers, dynamic memory allocation, file handling, searching and sorting algorithms.

---

## Basic Electrical Engineering Lab

Covers:
- DC motors, generators, and transformers
- AC motors
- Energy meters, power meters, voltage meters, ammeters
- Network theorems
- Electrically safe experimental environment

---

## Advanced Communication Skills Lab

For 3rd year B.Tech students, focused on:
- Fluency in spoken English
- Listening at normal conversational speed
- Vocabulary development
- Reading comprehension in different contexts
- Written communication
- Behavioural skills for personal and professional life
- Interview preparation and industry readiness
