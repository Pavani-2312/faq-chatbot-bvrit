**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/library/

## Overview

The BVRITH Library serves as the intellectual hub of BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women, fostering a strong culture of learning, research, and academic excellence.

Housed in a modern four-storey building known as the **Ruby Block**, the library is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. It maintains a comprehensive collection of textbooks, reference books, novels, encyclopaedias, magazines, and national and international journals.

The library also provides co-curricular and competitive examination resources, and has established the literary club **"PORTIA: The Literary Coven"** to nurture creativity, critical thinking, and communication skills.

The BVRITH Library is a proud member of:
- **DELNET** (Developing Library Network)
- **National Digital Library of India (NDLI)**

All materials are systematically organized using the **Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC)**.

## Library Automation

BVRITH library is automated with **Engineering College Automation Package (ECAP)**. The Library catalogue can be accessed through **OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue)**, which provides information of the library collection online.

The library also encourages students to access free content from the **AICTE-sponsored Ekumbh** website.

A **Book Bank** is maintained for SC/ST students, providing additional books offered by the Social Welfare Department, Government of Telangana.

---

## Digital Library

- 10 computers with internet facility
- Access to subscribed online content, NPTEL video lectures, and online mock tests
- E-journal packages — **IEEE-ASPP, J-GATE (JET)** — accessible through **KNIMBUS mLibrary** platform and the mLibrary mobile app

---

## Remote Access — Knimbus e-Library

BVRITH library provides remote access to its subscribed content through the **KNIMBUS platform**.

All staff and students can self-register with their institute mail ID. The Knimbus e-library is also accessible on smartphones via the **mLibrary** app (Google Play Store).

---

## e-Library (Federated Search)

BVRITH e-library uses a federated search engine to search all subscribed content simultaneously using keywords. Search results can be filtered by year, publication, and type. Training is provided at the digital library to use this facility.

---

## Plagiarism Check

BVRITH subscribes to **iThenticate** (a Turnitin product) for plagiarism checks for staff and students writing for publication.
- Requests: library@bvrithyderabad.edu.in
- Final year UG and PG projects are checked for plagiarism only when submitted through their guide/coordinator.
