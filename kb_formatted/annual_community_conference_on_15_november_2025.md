## Post Details

- **Published:** November 13, 2025
- **Categories:** News

## Summary

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women received the prestigious **"Best CSI Supporting Principal Award"** at the Annual Community Conference on 15 November 2025. The award was received by Principal Dr. K.V.N. Sunitha from the Computer Society of India (CSI), Hyderabad Chapter, recognising the institution's unwavering commitment to academic excellence, research, and practical applications of engineering principles.
