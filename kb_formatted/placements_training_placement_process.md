# Training and Placement Process

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/placements/training-placement-process/
**Category:** Placements

---

## Overview

BVRITH follows a structured, year-wise training process from the first year to the final year to make students placement-ready.

---

## Training Components

### 1. Diagnostic Test
A **Diagnostic Test** is conducted to assess the foundational skills of incoming first-year B.Tech students. It evaluates:
- Mathematics
- English
- Psychometric Testing
- Quantitative Aptitude
- Mental Ability

Results help faculty customize teaching strategies and provide focused support.

### 2. Basic Programming Skills
Enables students to leverage computers for innovative solutions, automate tasks, and solve complex problems. Strengthens critical thinking, logical reasoning, and problem-solving abilities.

### 3. Vishnu Student Success Centre (VSSC)
A holistic support system offering:
- Personalized tutoring
- Academic assistance
- Career mentorship (tailored to individual needs)
- Workshops on wellness, personal development, and confidence-building

### 4. Women in Software Engineering (WISE)
A distinctive initiative running alongside the regular academic curriculum. It prepares women engineering students for the global tech industry:
- Delivered in two levels
- Trains students in the latest technologies
- Makes students industry-ready and highly competitive

### 5. Web Application / Gaming Development Skills (Python)
Training in Python for:
- Web development using Django and Flask (rapid, scalable applications)
- Game development using Pygame and Panda3D (2D and 3D games)

### 6. Intellectual Learning in Engineering Applications (ILEA)
Focuses on:
- Mindset transformation and self-reflection
- Personality development and self-awareness for career success
- Identifying strengths and areas of improvement
- Psychometric assessments for personal and professional growth

### 7. Aptitude Training
Enhances natural abilities in:
- Logical reasoning
- Quantitative skills
- Problem-solving

Prepares students for aptitude tests and competitive examinations.

### 8. Entrepreneurship Awareness
- Encourages self-employment and business creation as viable career paths
- Introduces participants to the start-up ecosystem
- Promotes innovation, initiative, and self-reliance

### 9. Competitive Coding (3 Levels)
- Sharpens problem-solving abilities and coding efficiency
- Develops logical thinking and strong grasp of data structures and algorithms
- Essential for technical interviews and real-world software development

### 10. Industry Programs / Internships / Mentorships
- Multiple collaborations with industry partners
- Students can pursue certifications leading to internships and mentorship
- Bridges the gap between classroom learning and industry expectations

### 11. Alumni Support
Alumni contribute through:
- Mock interviews
- Motivational and technical talks
- Career guidance and networking opportunities
- Job referrals
