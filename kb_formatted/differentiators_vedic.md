## About VEDIC

**VEDIC (Vishnu Educational Development and Innovation Centre)** is a unique centre of excellence set up as a transformational platform for Sri Vishnu Educational Society institutions. Students and staff undergo residential learning experiences at this centre. VEDIC is involved in educational research to increase student success and promote active learning, with a mission to create inspiring teachers and innovative students.
