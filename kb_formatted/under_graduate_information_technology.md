## Overview

The B.Tech. in Information Technology (IT) at BVRIT HYDERABAD has an intake of 120 seats. Information Technology has an immense impact on modern-day life, creating huge demand in the sector. Around 55 students have been placed in multinational companies such as Capgemini, CSC, Accenture, and more.

## Course Highlights

- **Degree:** B.Tech. (4-year program offered by BVRIT-H)
- Ability to design and analyse any IT network comprising prevalent technologies within realistic constraints
- Trains students to function on multi-disciplinary teams, formulate and solve engineering problems, and design solutions for cumulative development
- Career domains: Networking Specialist, IT Engineer, and more
