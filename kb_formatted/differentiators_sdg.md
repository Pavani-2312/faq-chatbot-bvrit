## About SDG at BVRIT HYDERABAD

BVRIT HYDERABAD is committed to contributing to the United Nations' **17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)** adopted in 2015. The college has undertaken multiple eco-friendly initiatives aligned with the SDG framework.

## Green Initiatives

### Solar Plant
A **34 kWP Grid-Tied Rooftop Solar PV System** has been installed through the Jawaharlal Nehru National Solar Mission, facilitated by the Telangana State Renewable Energy Development Corporation Limited. Total project cost: **Rs. 17 lakh**.

### Sewage Water Treatment Plant
A state-of-the-art STP with a capacity of **60,000 litres per day (KLD)**, using advanced **Sequencing Batch Reactor (SBR)** technology. Recycled water is reused for gardening and cleaning.

### Rainwater Harvesting
**Four rainwater harvesting pits** with a total storage capacity of **55,394 litres**, integrated with the STP for optimised water management.

### Electric Vehicles
An **electric vehicle charging station** has been established on campus. The campus uses a fleet of electric vehicles, bikes, and bicycles for internal transportation, reducing carbon emissions.

### Energy-Efficient Buildings
The college Library features a glass-fronted façade that allows natural sunlight to illuminate the interior from approximately 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM, eliminating the need for artificial lighting during the day. BVRIT HYDERABAD is part of the **Energy Swaraj Foundation** and received the **Silver Certificate of Appreciation** for making 500+ people energy literate.

### Food Waste to Manure
An innovative system converts all organic food waste into **nutrient-rich compost** used in the college garden and landscaping.

### Vehicle Design Lab
A state-of-the-art **Vehicle Design Lab** enables students to design, develop, and test electric vehicles, with participation in national and international competitions.
