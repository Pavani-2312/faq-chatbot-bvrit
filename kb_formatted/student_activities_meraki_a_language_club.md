## About Meraki

**Meraki** (Greek: doing something with soul, creativity, or love) is the Language Club at BVRIT HYDERABAD. It provides a platform for students to learn language skills and management skills through simple techniques and fun learning.

**Motto:** Communicate to Connect

## Skills Developed

- **Communication:** Expressive skills — communicating effectively and winning the full attention of listeners
- **Leadership:** Strategic thinking, planning, persuasion, influence, people management, and change management
- **Time Management:** Making optimal use of the 24 hours available every day
- **Critical Thinking:** Making high-quality decisions that improve quality of life
- **Team Building:** Facilitating better communication and breaking barriers
- **Innovation:** Being creative and original in work and thinking
