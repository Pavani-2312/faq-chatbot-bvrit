## Overview

The B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering – Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSE AI&ML) specialisation prepares students for Industry 4.0 emerging and exponential technologies. The programme started with a sanctioned intake of 60 seats from AY 2020–2021.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) deals with the ability of machines to closely imitate intelligent human behaviour. Machine Learning (ML) enables machines to learn, grow, and develop on their own when provided new data. AI/ML applications like Siri, Alexa, Watson, Cortana, LinkedIn, and Google AI Assistant are part of everyday tasks — and demand for skilled AI/ML professionals is growing rapidly.

## Course Highlights

- **Degree:** B.Tech. (4-year program offered by BVRIT-H)
- Develops key understanding of Linear Algebra and Statistics as foundations of AI/ML
- Hands-on experience with machine learning components, intelligent reasoning, and AI tools & technologies
- Ability to design intelligent solutions in domains such as natural language processing, text mining, robotics, reasoning, and problem-solving
