## About IEI

The **Institution of Engineers (India) — IEI** is the national organisation of engineers in India, with more than one million members across 15 engineering disciplines in 125 centres and chapters in India and worldwide. It is the world's largest multi-disciplinary engineering professional society and is acclaimed for pioneering non-formal education in engineering.

## IEI Chapter at BVRIT HYDERABAD

BVRIT HYDERABAD holds both an Institute Membership and a Department Membership with IEI. The chapter organises events and technical activities to promote engineering excellence among students.
