## About IETE

The **Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE)** is India's leading professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, and IT. IETE conducts and sponsors technical meetings, conferences, symposia, exhibitions, and publishes technical journals. It provides continuing education and career advancement opportunities to its members.

## IETE Branch at BVRIT HYDERABAD

The IETE Student Branch at BVRIT HYDERABAD is active across multiple departments including ECE and EEE.
