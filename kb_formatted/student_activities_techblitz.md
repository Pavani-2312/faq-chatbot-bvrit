## About TechBlitz

**TechBlitz** is the vibrant technical association formed by the amalgamation of the Departments of CSE and IT. It was established in **2013**, starting with 125 student and faculty members.

TechBlitz helps students enrich their lives by hosting both technical and social events:

**Technical events:** Group discussions, technical demos, seminars, industry workshops, programming and gaming contests.

**Social events:** Workshops and weekend classes for school students, Fresher's welcome, farewells, and other campus events.

## Professional Bodies Under TechBlitz

- **CSI (Computer Society of India)** — Inaugurated with Membership No. M-10017 in 2018. All students of all branches are members.
- **IEEE Student Branch** — Approved 15 May 2014 (Branch Code: STB15591), inaugurated 12 Sep 2014 with twelve student members.
- **ISTE Student Branch** — Inaugurated with No. AP-175 and Institutional Membership IM2278 in 2013. All students of all branches are members.
