## About the College

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women is the youngest college under the umbrella of Sri Vishnu Educational Society. Established in 2012 with the intention of enabling women engineers to reach greater heights, it has acquired many accolades in academics, placements, and technical competitions. With exceptional results from the very first batch, BVRIT HYDERABAD is now one of the most sought-after institutes in Hyderabad for prospective engineering students.

**Tagline:** Empowering Women to Engineer a Better Future.
