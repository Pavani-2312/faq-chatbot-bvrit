## Overview

Sri Vishnu Educational Society is described with the theme "Shaping the future of education with passion and purpose." The society aims for continuous improvement in education and is committed to delivering world-class education in various fields.

## Inspiration

The founder chairman, Late Padma Bhushan Dr. B. V. Raju, established several educational institutions through his vision and foresight. These institutions contribute to shaping the future of students each year.

Dr. B. V. Raju also initiated organizations to serve society, including development programmes and the Dr. B. V. Raju Foundation for needy people. The foundation distributed house sites to poor people.

## Social Contributions

Dr. B. V. Raju Foundation and Sri Vishnu Educational Society constructed:

- Two high schools
- Three elementary schools
- Four mahila mandals
- One kalyanamandapam
- Veterinary hospitals in several villages

The page states these were constructed without monetary help from government or private sources.

## BVRIT HYDERABAD

BVRIT HYDERABAD is the youngest engineering college established under Sri Vishnu Educational Society. It was established to promote engineering education for girls in Telangana.
