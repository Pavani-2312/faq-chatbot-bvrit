## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD has highly committed faculty with strong academic and research backgrounds. The institute's R&D strength is demonstrated through:

- Sponsored projects granted by **DST, NRSC, NIMAT, Microsoft, MSME, UGC-DAE**
- Research papers published in **SCI/SCOPUS** indexed journals
- Faculty and student research projects monitored by lead coordinators in each department

## Research Areas (Thrust Areas)

The institute has identified research thrust areas enabling faculty and students to choose research problems across departments including:

- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Cybersecurity and Blockchain
- Data Science and Healthcare
- VLSI and Communications
- Renewable Energy Systems
- Signal Processing
- Internet of Things
- Natural Language Processing
- Power Systems and Electric Vehicles
- Chemistry (Organic, Medicinal, Environmental)
