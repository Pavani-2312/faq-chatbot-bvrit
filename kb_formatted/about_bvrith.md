## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women (BVRITH) is the youngest offspring of Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES), founded by Chairman Sri K V Vishnu Raju in 2012.

Established in 2012 with the clear Vision & Mission to empower women to aspire for a better career, the college was accredited by NAAC with Grade 'A' in the year 2020 and granted UGC autonomous status from AY 2023-24 for a period of 10 years. The Institute is duly approved by AICTE, Government of Telangana and is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH).

## Programs Offered

- CSE AI&ML (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) — 120 seats
- EEE (Electrical and Electronics Engineering) — 60 seats

## Vision

> To emerge as the best among the institutes of technology and research in the country dedicated to the cause of promoting quality technical education.

## Mission

At BVRITH, we strive to:
- Achieve academic excellence through innovative learning practices
- Enhance intellectual ability and technical competency for a successful career
- Encourage research and innovation
- Nurture students towards holistic development with emphasis on leadership skills, life skills and human values

## Core Values

1. **Holistic Development** — Believes in holistic development through sports, social service, cultural activities, internships and mentorships with industry
2. **Excellence in Education** — Faculty trained in scientific educational practices; student-centric approach results in excellent outcome-based learning
3. **Women Empowerment** — Established with the belief "Educate a woman and you educate a family"; strives to create future women leaders
4. **Integrity** — Pursues ethical practices, sticks to the law of the land, always fair to Staff, Students and other stakeholders
5. **Social Responsibility** — Contributes to the immediate neighborhood through various means and activities
6. **Accountability and Transparency** — Transparency maintained across all levels; everyone encouraged to understand their responsibilities
7. **Freedom of Expression** — Everyone encouraged to express their opinions and suggestions openly without inhibitions
