## Overview

The B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at BVRIT HYDERABAD has an intake of 120 seats. ECE is an extensively progressing field with new ideas emerging constantly — from mobile phones to fibre optics and remote sensing. Every year, 75%+ students get placed in companies like Qualcomm, John Deere, Adept Chips, Capgemini, TCS, NCR, NTT Data, Torry Harris, Wipro, Optum, Infosys, and Deloitte. Some students pursue higher studies (MS, M.Tech, MBA) at reputed institutions worldwide.

## Course Highlights

- **Degree:** B.Tech. (4-year program offered by BVRIT-H)
- Ability to design and analyse any electronic circuit comprising prevalent technologies within realistic constraints
- Trains students to function on multi-disciplinary teams, formulate and solve engineering problems
- Career domains: Telecom Engineering, Circuit Design, Process Management, Electronics Engineering, and more
