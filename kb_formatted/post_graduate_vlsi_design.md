## Programme Overview

The M.Tech in VLSI Design focuses on the design, development and optimization of Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) systems. The programme covers integrated circuits and systems-on-chip at physical and logical levels.

## Career Opportunities Listed

- VLSI Design Engineer
- ASIC Design Engineer
- FPGA Design Engineer
- Verification Engineer

- HDL and FPGA design tools

- System-on-Chip design tools
- Scripting tools
