## Overview

The National Service Scheme (NSS) unit at BVRITH was established in 2013 under Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad. It provides opportunities for students to contribute to community and national service while developing personality and leadership traits.

## Motto

The motto of the National Service Scheme is **"Not Me But You"**. It expresses democratic living, selfless service, appreciation of others' points of view, and consideration for fellow human beings.

## Aim

The aim of NSS is the development of students' personalities through community service.

## Objectives

- Work with and among people
- Engage in creative and constructive social action
- Enhance knowledge of individuals and the community
- Develop democratic leadership skills
- Develop program organizing skills
- Support self-employment development
- Bridge connectivity between the educated and the uneducated
- Promote willingness to serve weaker sections of the community

## Faith of NSS

The NSS page emphasizes faith in people’s ability to take responsibility, build self-confidence, and participate in nation-building activities.

## NSS Unit Activities

The BVRITH NSS unit works for community welfare through activities such as:

- Daan-Utsav
- Blood donation
- Wall of Kindness
- Teaching at government schools
- Social service activities
- Environment-friendly activities

## Social Purpose

The NSS program aims to help people in need, improve standards of living, and support dignified living. The page describes NSS as beneficial to both students and society.
