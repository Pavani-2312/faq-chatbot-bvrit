## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD started a **Student Yoga Program** in the year **2015** to support student health, fitness, and cognitive well-being. The program runs under the guidance of yoga teacher **Dr. P. Nagaraju** and Faculty Coordinator **Ms. B. Rajitha**.

Students use this platform to obtain relief from the regular academic schedule and stress. The program provides a double benefit — a healthy stress-free life, along with valid certification.

---

## Certification

Every year, a **Yoga Certification Examination** is conducted. Marks are awarded based on:
- Attendance
- Theory examination
- Practical examination

## The Eight-Fold Path of Yoga (Asthang Yoga by Patanjali)

Patanjali established yoga as a precise discipline with eight essential practices:

1. **Yam** — the five rules of moral restraint
2. **Niyam** — the five rules of moral observance
3. **Asan** — the practice of Yoga postures
4. **Pranayam** — the practice of breath control
5. **Pratyahar** — the withdrawal of mind from the senses
6. **Dharana** — the practice of mental focusing
7. **Dhyan** — the practice of meditation
8. **Samadhi** — the state of super consciousness and perfect equanimity

## Why Yoga for College Students

Yoga helps students:
- Manage stress and remain focused
- Maintain physical and emotional health
- Find balance between academics, social life, and personal well-being
- Build a positive state of mind
- Cope with exam pressure, peer pressure, and long study hours
