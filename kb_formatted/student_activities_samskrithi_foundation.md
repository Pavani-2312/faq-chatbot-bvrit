## Overview

Samskruti Foundation strives towards nation building by promoting and nurturing Indian culture, traditions, and quality of life through various outreach programmes. It promotes cultural identity by involving the intelligentsia of society in inspiring, educating, and encouraging people to uphold Indian culture, traditions, and values.

## Vision

To be a National Resource Centre for Culture.

## Mission

To preserve, protect, and encourage the practice of Bharatiya culture and ethos through research, education, training, and dialogue.

## Objectives

- Serve society as a whole without any distinction of caste, class, or creed; eliminate all types of social evils
- Create social consciousness and civic sense; promote harmony and integration of diverse communities
- Focus on creating a value-based ecosystem in young minds through Cultural Leadership Centres (CLC)
- Create manpower resources ecosystem (voluntary workers, full-time and part-time) for outreach
- Conduct research activities through Samskruti Adyayana Kendra (SAK) on socio, cultural, political, spiritual, and economic challenges
- Highlight the significance, importance, and contributions of ancient Indian dignitaries in Science & Technology, Economics, Public Administration, and other areas
