## About IIC

The **Institution's Innovation Council (IIC)** is an initiative of MoE's Innovation Cell, Government of India, to promote Innovation, Start-up, and Entrepreneurship activities in higher educational institutions.

BVRIT HYDERABAD registered for IIC in **September 2019**.

- **Vice President:** Dr. K. V. N. Sunitha, Principal
- **Convenor:** Dr. J. Naga Vishnu Vardhan
- **IIC 2.0 Star Rating:** 4.5 out of 5
- **Recognition:** BVRIT HYDERABAD was recognised as a **Mentor Institute**

Faculty and students actively participate in programs conducted by MIC (MoE's Innovation Cell) and IIC, and the college also conducts related innovation and entrepreneurship activities independently.
