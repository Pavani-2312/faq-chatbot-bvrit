## Dr. K. V. N. Sunitha — Principal, BVRIT HYDERABAD

Dr. K. V. N. Sunitha is the **Founder Principal** of BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women, Hyderabad, serving since **August 2012**.

### Qualifications

- B.Tech in ECE — Acharya Nagarjuna University (ANU)
- M.Tech in CS — REC Warangal (1993)
- Ph.D in CSE — JNTU Hyderabad (2006)

### Experience

- **30+ years** of Teaching Experience
- **15+ years** of Research Experience

### Research Areas

- Natural Language Processing
- Speech Processing
- Network & Web Security

### Publications & Books

Authored 5 textbooks:
1. *Programming in UNIX and Compiler Design* — BS Publications, 2008
2. *Automata Theory* — Tata McGrawHill, 2010
3. *Theory of Computation* — TMH, 2011
4. *Compiler Construction* — Pearson India Ltd, July 2013
5. *Formal Languages and Automata Theory* — Pearson India Ltd, 2015

- Published **185+ papers** in International & National Journals and conferences
- **7 patents**
- Guided **20 Ph.D scholars**

### Research Funding
