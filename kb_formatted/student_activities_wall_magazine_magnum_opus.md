## About Magnum Opus

**MAGNUM OPUS** is the College Wall Magazine at BVRIT HYDERABAD. It showcases students' imagination, creativity, and artistic expression — celebrating writing, visual art, and meaningful creative work by students.

> "Nothing sublimely artistic has ever arisen out of mere art… There must always be a rich moral soil for any artistic growth." — G.K. Chesterton
