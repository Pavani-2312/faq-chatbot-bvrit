## About FOSS Club

The **FOSS Club** at BVRIT HYDERABAD promotes **Free and Open-Source Software (FOSS)** — software that users can freely use, copy, study, and modify, with source code openly shared for voluntary improvement.

## Benefits of FOSS

- Decreased software costs
- Increased security and stability (especially against malware)
- Protection of privacy
- More control over hardware by users

Free, open-source operating systems such as Linux and BSD descendants power millions of servers, desktops, smartphones (e.g. Android), and other devices globally.

The club gives students hands-on experience with open-source tools and encourages contributions to the global open-source community.
