## Overview

Vishnu School of Music is a platform under Sri Vishnu Educational Society for students to learn music and develop creative and communication skills.

## Training Areas

Students can learn:

- Playback singing
- Acoustic guitar
- Keyboard
- Percussion

## Activities

Sri Vishnu Educational Society has a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with SIFM (School of Indian Film Music), Chennai. The collaboration trains students in playback singing and acoustic guitar under Dr. Divakar Subramaniam, Founder and Director of SIFM.

## Playback Singing

Playback singing is pre-recorded singing used in movies. Playback singers record songs for soundtracks, while actors or actresses lip-sync the songs on screen.

## Acoustic Guitar

An acoustic guitar produces sound by transmitting string vibrations through the air. The guitar body resonates with the string vibrations to create sound.
