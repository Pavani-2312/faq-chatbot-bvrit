## About the Cultural Club

**Jhankar** is the Cultural Club at BVRIT HYDERABAD, operating under the **Saarang** umbrella. The club aims to mould the personality of students and expose them to a wide range of cultural events throughout the year. It develops intellectual, organisational, and social skills — including leadership, teamwork, accepting responsibility, and decision-making.

Faculty Coordinators: **Dr. V. Malathi** and **Ms. A. Ramya**

## Objectives

- Enhance skills and accentuate students' understanding and learning
- Allow students to express themselves while enlightening their peers
- Provide a platform for students to share imagination and talent
- Connect with other institutes of the university through cultural activities

## Events Organised

- Annual Day Celebrations
- Festival Celebrations
- Freshers Day
- Women's Day
- Talent Hunt
- Musical Concert by SPICMACAY
- Induction Day
- Alumni Meet
- Cultural Events at NAAC Inspection
- Graduation Day
- Hostel Day and Hostel Farewell Day
