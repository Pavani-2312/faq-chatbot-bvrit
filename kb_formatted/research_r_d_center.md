## About the R&D Center

The **R&D Center** at BVRIT HYDERABAD aims to advance knowledge and practice of modern evidence-based impactful research. Its purpose is to enable and strengthen research aspirations among faculty, scholars, and students — enhancing research innovation, academic excellence, real-world problem-solving, and knowledge dissemination.

The center aids faculty in publishing research through journal publications, patents, conference papers, and books, while continuously exploring new research avenues to keep the institution's research pipeline active.
