## Overview

The **Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)** was established in **2012** with an intake of 60 students, affiliated to JNTUH. The department was **accredited by NBA** in AY 2018-19 for three years.

## Faculty

Headed by **Dr. B. Srinivasa Rao** (M.Tech., Ph.D.), Senior Member of IEEE, FIE, and LMISTE. Research areas: power system optimization, renewable energy systems, multi-objective optimization, deregulated power systems, optimal operation with FACTS devices.

## Vision

> To develop comprehensively trained and socially responsible women electrical and electronics engineers with competencies and capabilities to adapt to new challenges.

## Mission

- **M1:** Empower students adept at latest technologies by providing innovative learning environment
- **M2:** Cultivate interdisciplinary research mindset to develop engineering solutions
- **M3:** Inculcate ethical behavior and professional attitude to embrace holistic concept of living

## Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

- **PEO1:** Propose effective solutions for complex EEE problems using modern techniques
- **PEO2:** Excel in career and compete with global peers in Techno-Scientific fields
- **PEO3:** Exhibit good communication skills, ethical behavior and social perception
- **PEO4:** Stimulate economic growth and job opportunities through entrepreneurship

## Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

- **PSO-1:** Apply fundamental knowledge to analyze and implement solutions for societal challenges
- **PSO-2:** Attain competence in using novel tools for design and analysis of grid-connected renewable energy systems
