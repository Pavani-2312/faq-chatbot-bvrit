# NASSCOM Activities at BVRIT HYDERABAD

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/differentiators/nasscom/
**Category:** Differentiators

## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD has executed the following MoUs between Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES) and the National Association of Software and Service Companies (NASSCOM):

- NASSCOM Sector Skills Council Qualification Pack
- Non-Disclosure Agreement for QP Content Sharing
- Acceptance of Proposal and Expression of Interest of Future Skills
- Future Skills Subscription Agreement

Three NASSCOM training initiatives are active at BVRIT HYDERABAD, with faculty members trained by industry experts under the Train-The-Trainer (TTT) program at VEDIC.

## NASSCOM Future Skills

SVES initiated the NASSCOM Future Skills Program in **May 2019** in collaboration with NASSCOM and Edcast Asia Pvt Ltd, Mumbai.

**Ten future skills identified:**
3D Printing · Artificial Intelligence · Big Data Analytics · Blockchain Technology · Cloud Computing · Cyber Security · Internet of Things · Mobile Technologies · Robotics Process Automation · Virtual Reality

**Milestones:**
- 22 Dec 2019: Faculty CuraThon to create content on the Future Skills platform
- 210 students of all branches and 18 faculty provided platform logins
- 70 students completed foundation courses; 113 progressing on AI, Big Data Analytics, and IoT courses

## AWS Educate (via NASSCOM)

NASSCOM in collaboration with Amazon initiated the **AWS Educate** program at SVES. Students gain access to cloud computing career pathways including:
Machine Learning · Data Science · Application Development · DevOps · Cyber Security · Solution Architecture · Cloud Support

More than **180 students** on-boarded. Faculty mentors from BVRIT HYDERABAD guide students on virtual classroom creation, project assignments, and credits.

## NASSCOM Webinars Conducted

Key webinars attended by students include topics such as Cybersecurity, AI, Blockchain, IoT, Big Data Analytics, RPA, Edge Computing, and Virtual Interview preparation (2019–2020).
