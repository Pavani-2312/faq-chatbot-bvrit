## About the Art Club

The Art Club at BVRIT HYDERABAD was initiated in **2022** under the leadership of Principal Dr. K.V.N. Sunitha to encourage students' inner talent. It serves as a stress buster, improves inner talent, and fosters entrepreneurial skills.

## Activities

The Art Club covers:
- Guitar playing and singing
- Western dance and classical dance
- Painting
- Therapeutic weaving (bangle decoration, weaving competitions)
- Organic farming (under the Nature Club)

## Nature Club (Sub-initiative)

Under the Art Club, the **Nature Club** teaches students how to reduce and reuse waste, prepare manure from waste to grow plants, and sensitises students to the importance of maintaining an eco-friendly environment.
