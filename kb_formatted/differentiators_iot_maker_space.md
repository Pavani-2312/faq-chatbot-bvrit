## About IoT Maker Space

**IoT Maker Space** is an initiative by the Telangana Academy for Skill and Knowledge (TASK) and Hyderabad Software Enterprises Association (HYSEA) extended to engineering colleges. It aims to develop students' technical skills, presentation skills, innovative thinking, and prototype development, preparing them as future entrepreneurs.

BVRIT HYDERABAD is a registered IoT Maker Space college, supported by **UPTEC IDEA Labs** as Industry Mentor. A specialised lab with equipment worth **Rs. 5 lakhs** has been established.

Every summer, the prestigious **JNTUH EXCITE** event (45 days) is organised as part of this initiative.
