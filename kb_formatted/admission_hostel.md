## Overview

BVRITH hostel buildings are located right within the college campus. The hostel is 100% ragging-free with an anti-ragging squad constantly monitoring.

## Key Facts

- 4 blocks of hostels
- 150+ rooms
- 500+ occupancy capacity
- Common room available for students
- Gym area within hostel
- Experienced lady wardens and security officers in each block

## Facilities

- 24/7 water supply
- Geyser systems
- Nutritious food provided
- Television for entertainment and current affairs
- Full-time lady physical director conducting outdoor games — volleyball, badminton, throw-ball
- Eco-friendly surroundings
- Fresh choice bakery
- Fast food center
- Yoga & sports

## About Hostel Life

The hostel atmosphere is conducive to study and group discussions. Students can elevate their talents in painting, dancing, singing and many more activities. The hostel experience grooms overall personality — making students better team-players, decision makers, and adaptable individuals. Every festival is celebrated grandly, making it feel like home away from home.
