## About EDC

The **Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC)** at BVRIT HYDERABAD was established on **8 July 2015**, evolving from an Incubation Centre started on 21 December 2013. Its main objective is to create an entrepreneurial culture and foster innovation among students and faculty. EDC operates alongside the **Vishnu Startup and Accelerator Centre for Women (V-SAW)**.
