## Overview

The **Department of Basic Sciences and Humanities (BS&H)** lays a strong foundation for all core engineering courses. It handles courses for first-year students across all departments covering:

- Engineering Drawing
- English (Theory & ELCS Lab + Advanced Communication Skills Lab)
- Mathematics and Mathematical Methods
- Engineering Physics (Theory & Lab)
- Engineering Chemistry (Theory & Lab)
- Environmental Studies
- Management Science
- Engineering Workshop
- Fluid Mechanics and Hydraulic Machinery Lab

## Objectives

- Impart value-based knowledge to students
- Keep pace with growing needs of society and industry; prepare students for life
- Organize training programs, seminars, and workshops to upgrade staff performance
- Develop research activity and publish technical papers in National and International Journals

## Manifesto

The department strives to lay an excellent basic foundation in Science and Humanities, increasing students' knowledge, skills, and ability — helping them comprehend core engineering subjects and building a brilliant career.
