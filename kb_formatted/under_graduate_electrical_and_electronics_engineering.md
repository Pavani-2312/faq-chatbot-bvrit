## Overview

The B.Tech. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) at BVRIT HYDERABAD has an intake of 60 seats. EEE is a continuously evolving discipline where the difficulty of engineering challenges increases exponentially with advancement in technology. Around 37 students have been placed in bluechip and multinational companies such as Bosch, Capgemini, ADP, Tech Mahindra, and more.

## Course Highlights

- **Degree:** B.Tech. (4-year program offered by BVRIT-H)
- Ability to design and analyse any electronic circuit comprising prevalent technologies within realistic constraints
- Trains students to function on multi-disciplinary teams, formulate and solve engineering problems, and design solutions for cumulative development
