# Training and Placement Cell

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/placements/training-and-placement-cell/
**Category:** Placements

---

## Overview

The central placement cell under **Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES)** facilitates students of BVRIT Hyderabad, BVRIT Narsapur, SVECW Bhimavaram, and VIT Bhimavaram for placements. Students of BVRIT Hyderabad are highly appreciated by employers from corporate and public sectors, and have consistently topped campus placement records among private institutions in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh for the last seven years.

The college placement team, under the guidance of the central TAP Cell, facilitates students by offering advice, guidance, information, and job-seeking support. Career Advisors are available to discuss ideas and plans, and students are encouraged to attend talks, information fairs, and recruitment events.

---

## Functions of the Training and Placement (TAP) Cell

- Arranges in-plant industrial training for students during summer and intra-semester breaks
- Encourages students to become entrepreneurs through Entrepreneurship Development Programmes
- Promotes career guidance and counselling through lectures by senior corporate executives and visiting professors
- Provides training to face competitive exams like GATE, GRE, GMAT, TOEFL and prepares students for interviews
- Organizes campus interviews for pre-final and final year students with industrial and business houses across India
- Interacts with Industry to enhance employment opportunities and to get projects for staff to improve their real-world awareness

---

## Structure

The Training and Placement Cell is headed by a **Dean** and is assisted by Placement Officers.
