## Overview

The **Department of Computer Science and Engineering – Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSE-AIML)** was established in **2020** with an initial intake of 60 students. Intake increased to 120 from the academic year 2025-26. Current strength: 180 students.

## Faculty Strength

- **Total Faculty:** 28 (5 Professors, 5 Associate Professors, 18 Assistant Professors)

## Intake History

| Year | Intake |
|------|--------|
| 2020–21 (established) | 60 |
| 2025–26 | 120 |

## Programme Highlights

- Best teaching and learning practices in AI and ML
- Industry-oriented curriculum with hands-on industry projects
- Regular sessions by industry experts
- Advanced topics: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Pattern Recognition, Computer Vision, Cognitive Computing, Human-Computer Interaction, Generative AI, Sustainable AI
- Internships in top companies: Amazon, Microsoft, Optum, PwC, Hexagon
- Industry projects with companies like Hexagon (Deep Learning and ML)
- Courses from Simplilearn, TalentSprint, Coursera, LinkedIn
- Advanced labs with modern software and digital classroom resources

## Vision

> Produce competent technocrats, researchers, and entrepreneurs in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning to build an ecosystem that significantly contributes to the nation.

## Mission

- **M1:** Impart skills through various learning methodologies and value-added courses to be technically competent
- **M2:** Build the research culture through participation in innovative projects and publications
- **M3:** Inculcate ethics, leadership skills, life skills, and lifelong learning
- **M4:** Expose students to real-time environment through internships and mentorships via industry collaborations

## Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

- **PEO-1:** Adapt emerging technologies to contribute to technical innovations for progressive development
- **PEO-2:** Productively engage in multidisciplinary research areas by applying basic engineering science principles
- **PEO-3:** Demonstrate strong technical skills to create novel designs/products to address social & environmental issues
- **PEO-4:** Exhibit professional attitude, teamwork and practice code of ethics

## Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

- **PSO 1:** Ability to apply learned skills to build optimized solutions pertaining to Computer & Communication Systems, Data Processing, and Artificial Intelligence
- **PSO 2:** Employ standard strategies and practices in project development using FOSS (Free & Open-Source Software)
