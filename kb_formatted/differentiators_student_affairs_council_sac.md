## About SAC

The **Student Affairs Council (SAC)** of BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women is a landmark student-governed body established in **2025**. It is the **only council of its kind** across the entire Sri Vishnu Educational Society family, making BVRIT HYDERABAD the first SVES institution to introduce a formal, student-elected representative system.

The SAC was founded through the vision and support of Principal **Dr. K.V.N. Sunitha**, with strong mentorship from:

- **Dr. K.V.N. Sunitha** — Principal
- **Dr. Ch. Sunil Kumar** — Dean of Student Affairs & Vice Principal
- **Dr. V. Malathi** — Faculty Incharge

## Council Composition

The council comprises **10 elected members**:
President · Vice President · Secretary · Joint Secretary · Treasurer · Five Student Members (from all years and branches)

## Purpose

- Serve as the official voice of students
- Bridge student concerns, ideas, and aspirations with the administration
- Create an inclusive, vibrant campus environment through student-led initiatives
- Uphold values of responsibility, integrity, and excellence

## Key Achievement — First Tenure

Within **three months** of formation, the SAC successfully organised **Synergia** — a grand three-day college fest featuring technical events, cultural programmes, Annual Day celebrations, and inter-college participation. Synergia set a new benchmark for student-led initiatives at BVRIT HYDERABAD.

## Second Tenure

The second tenure commenced with an oath ceremony on **17 November**, with newly elected members pledging to uphold institutional values and represent the student body faithfully.
