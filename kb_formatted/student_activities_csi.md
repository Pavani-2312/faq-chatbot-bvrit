## About CSI

The **Computer Society of India (CSI)** is the largest and most professionally managed association for IT professionals in India. It is a non-profit professional society to exchange views, share ideas, and advance the theory and practice of computer science and IT.

## CSI Branch at BVRIT HYDERABAD

The BVRIT HYDERABAD CSI Student Branch was established in **2013**:

- **Original Membership No:** M10017 (valid till 30 June 2021)
- **Renewed Membership No:** 5024220017 (valid till 5 June 2029)

Under CSI, the IT and CSE departments regularly organise workshops, seminars, project exhibitions (main, mini, hobby), blind coding, paper presentations, skill tests, Java skill tests, technical talks, and professional development programs.
