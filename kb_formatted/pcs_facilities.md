# PCS Facilities (Physically Challenged Students)

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/pcs-facilities/
**Category:** Campus Facilities

---

## Overview

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women has created full-fledged facilities to support and meet the needs of **Physically Challenged Persons (Divyangjan)**.

## Available Facilities

- Lifts
- Ramps
- Washrooms for Divyangjan
- Wheelchair
- EV Vehicle
- Wall Stick
