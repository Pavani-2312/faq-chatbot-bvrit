## About VSSC

The **Vishnu Student Success Centre (VSSC)** offers a comprehensive range of resources to support all aspects of student development. It is open to all students within Sri Vishnu Educational Society — from first-year students adjusting to college life through to seniors preparing for professional careers.

**SPOC:** Ms. Nella Sowmya, Assistant Professor (sowmya.n@bvrithyderabad.edu.in)

## Focus Areas

- **Academic Support:** Personalised tutoring, subject-focused recap sessions, writing skills improvement
- **Networking & Connections:** Building meaningful professional relationships and collaboration opportunities
- **Personal Growth:** Mindful practices, self-discovery, and personal development workshops
- **Career Counselling:** Assessing career options, actionable steps, professional goal-setting
- **Wellness:** Zumba, digital detox, and healthy lifestyle programmes
- **Corporate Readiness:** Professional etiquettes, corporate skills, and confidence-building for the workplace

## Services

- Personalised tutoring and academic advising
- Career counselling tailored to individual needs
- Wellness and mental health workshops
- Professional development and progression workshops
