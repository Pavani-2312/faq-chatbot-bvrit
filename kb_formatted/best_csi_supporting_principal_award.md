# Best CSI Supporting Principal Award

**Category:** General

---

## Post Details

- **Published:** June 26, 2025
- **Categories:** Awards, News

## Summary

BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women, Hyderabad received the prestigious **"Best CSI Supporting Principal Award"**. The award was received by Principal Dr. K.V.N. Sunitha from the Computer Society of India (CSI), Hyderabad Chapter. This recognition is a tribute to the institution for its unwavering commitment in fostering a culture of academic excellence, research, and the practical applications of engineering principles.
