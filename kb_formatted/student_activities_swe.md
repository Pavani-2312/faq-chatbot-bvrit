## About SWE

The **Society of Women Engineers (SWE)** is a global nonprofit organisation founded in 1950, dedicated to supporting and advancing women in engineering and technology through scholarships, mentorship, professional development, networking, and outreach programs while promoting diversity and inclusion worldwide.

## SWE Student Chapter at BVRIT HYDERABAD

The SWE Student Chapter was established in **2025** with the objective of empowering women engineering students through professional development and leadership opportunities.

The chapter regularly organises:
- Technical workshops and seminars
- Distinguished and Eminent Speaker Programs
- Mentoring sessions and outreach activities
- Hackathons and innovation events
- Design thinking events and coding competitions
- Engineers' Day celebrations and weekly skill-development activities

## Benefits of SWE Membership

- Professional development — leadership training, workshops, and skill-building programs
- Mentorship — access to mentors and role models from academia and industry
- Networking — connect with engineers, leaders, and recruiters worldwide
- Scholarships and awards — financial support and recognition for academic excellence
- Career support — job resources, resume building, and career fairs
- Community and advocacy — promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion in engineering
- Global exposure — conferences, outreach programs, and international connections
