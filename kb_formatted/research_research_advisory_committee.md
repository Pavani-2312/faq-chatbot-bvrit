# Research Advisory Committee

**Source:** https://bvrithyderabad.edu.in/research/research-advisory-committee/
**Category:** Research

---

## Overview

The Research Advisory Committee (RAC) provides strategic guidance, oversight and recommendations to enhance the research capabilities of the institution. It supports innovation, research excellence and interdisciplinary collaboration.

## Objectives

- Advise on the institution's research strategy, priorities and goals.
- Identify emerging research areas and recommend initiatives to develop institutional research capacity.
- Provide feedback on research proposals, projects and funding applications.
- Foster collaborations with external research organizations, government bodies and industry partners.
- Ensure ethical standards are upheld in all research activities.
