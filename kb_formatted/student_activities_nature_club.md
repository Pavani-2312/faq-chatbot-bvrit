## About the Nature Club

The **Nature Club** at BVRIT HYDERABAD aims to cultivate an appreciation of nature among students and encourage participation in conservation initiatives. The club creates awareness about nature and environmental issues among students, faculty, and staff.

## Objectives

- Sensitise and mobilise students for preservation and conservation of nature
- Bring students closer to nature and highlight the importance of eco-friendly environments
- Encourage responsibility towards leaving the planet better for future generations

## Events Conducted

- Awareness session: Save Water, Save Earth
- Workshop on Cloth Bag Making
- Eco-Friendly Ganesh Making
- World Environment Day celebrations
