## Overview

The Alumni section of BVRIT HYDERABAD connects graduates through the SVES Global Alumni Digital Network and recognises Distinguished Alumni across all departments.

## Alumni Network

- **SVES Global Alumni Digital Network** — connects BVRIT HYDERABAD graduates globally
- **Distinguished Alumni** recognised from all departments:
  - CSE (Computer Science and Engineering)
  - IT (Information Technology)
  - ECE (Electronics and Communication Engineering)
  - EEE (Electrical and Electronics Engineering)
