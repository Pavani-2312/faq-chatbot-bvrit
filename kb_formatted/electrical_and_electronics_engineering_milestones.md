## Key Milestones (Chronological)

- **Inception** — Department established with intake of 60 students
- **Establishment of Deeptha** — Department Technical Association
- **Inauguration** of ISTE and IE(I) Student Chapters
- **MoU** with Daximac Solutions & VVE Transformers
- **NBA Accreditation** — Accredited for three years from AY 2018-19
- **Ms. D. Sowmya** — received JNTUH University Gold Medal (2019)
- **DST Project** — "Remote Diagnosis for Assisted Living Using IoT for Rural Areas" (₹33,03,600)
- **DST Project** — "Demonstration of MW Scale Solar Energy Integration in Weak Grid using D-SIDES" (₹48,20,964)
- **Faculty and students** invited as speakers at FOSSASIA, Singapore
- **Grid-Connected Roof Top Solar PV System Plant (34 KWp)** deployed on campus
