About HOD
Dr. Nagesh Deevi
Associate Professor & HoD
Nagesh Deevi
(Member, IEEE) received the Ph.D. degree in RF-VLSI from NIT Warangal. He is currently an Associate Professor with the Department of ECE, BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women. He has seven years of teaching experience and three years of research experience. He has a total of 28 research publications in international journals and international conferences and five patents published. His current research interests include device modeling, RF communications, on-chip component design, and semiconductor packaging.
