TITLE: Literary Club – BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

Literary Club
A
literary club
is a vibrant hub of creativity and intellectual growth, offering a unique platform for students to explore and enhance their literary talents. BVRIT HYDERABAD Literary Club provides a platform for students to express themselves creatively through writing, storytelling, and enacting.
Objectives
To cultivate a love for reading, encourage students to investigate a wide variety of literary genres and writers.
To develop students’ writing, analytical, and critical thinking abilities through discussions, debates, conversations, and peer reviews.
To hone their LSRW SKILLS and ingrain in them an interest for oration, stage performances and writing.
