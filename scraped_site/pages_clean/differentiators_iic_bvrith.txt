IIC BVRITH
“To the Optimist the glass is half full. To the pessimist the glass is half empty. To the engineer the glass is twice as big as it needs to be.”
The Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC) is one of the Unique Initiatives of MoE’s Innovation Cell, Govt. of India to promote Innovation, Start-up and Entrepreneurship activities. BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women registered for IIC in September 2019 to promote the vision of IIC in terms of Innovation, Start-up and Entrepreneurship among faculty members and students. Dr K V N Sunitha, Principal of BVRITH is the Vice – President of IIC BVRITH and Dr J Naga Vishnu Vardhan is the convenor. College got star rating of 4.5 out of 5 in IIC 2.0.College was recognized as a mentor Institute.  Faculty and Students actively take part in various programs conducted by MIC, IIC and college also conducts the related activities on its own.
