Transportation
Our institution believes that efficient ways of transportation in turn results in better productivity.
Thus BVRIT provides
15
buses for private transportation from all over the city to the campus. From over
15
different routes , the fleet carries students across Hyderabad and nearby to the campus. All the buses are equipped with GPRS system for tracking and safety of the students. The timings and stops are detailed below.
