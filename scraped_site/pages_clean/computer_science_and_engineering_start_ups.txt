About
Biopots for Nature is an innovative startup focused on converting agro-waste into eco-friendly alternatives to single-use plastic. Founded by student innovator A. Srija in 2022, the initiative produces biodegradable plant pots using groundnut shells. These biopots decompose naturally into nutrient-rich compost, promoting sustainable farming while reducing plastic waste. With over 20,000 units sold and three regular institutional customers, the project has generated substantial revenue and positive environmental impact. Field trials and tech validation were conducted with horticultural departments. Srija now seeks funding for advanced machinery to scale production and diversify into other biodegradable products like plates and Vinayaka idols.
