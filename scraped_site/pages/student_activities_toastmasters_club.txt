Toastmasters Club
“Life is not a problem to be solved, but a reality to be experienced
.”–
Soren Kierkegaard. Our Toastmasters’ club, a great platform, gives a real life  experience  in communication, leadership  and networking, which are the essential three pillars for  personal and  profession growth.
BVRIT HYDERABAD TM Club was chartered on Jun 19, 2017 with 32 members and it conducted 230 meetings so far. Toastmasters International provides a supportive and positive learning experience in which members develop communication, leadership skills and boost self-confidence and personal growth.
FEATURES:
The club conducts meeting every week on Saturday from 2-4 PM, to improvise required skills for members’ personal and profession life.
Every meeting will have three segments:
Prepared speeches, according to 11 educational modules
Impromptus speeches
Evaluation
Toastmasters acquire the following skills
Public speaking
Interpersonal
communication
Management
Strategic leadership
Conflict resolution
Coaching
Leadership
Speech writing
Project management
Motivational leadership
Motivational communication
Leading in complex situations
Networking
Interview skills
Collaborative leadership
As a result of meticulous planning and regular active participation in club activities, all Toastmasters   are being placed ever year in prestigious multinational companies such as Capgemini, Microsoft, Virtusa, Cone Labs, Tech Mahindra, etc.
