Library
The library is the temple of learning, and learning has liberated more people than all the wars in history
-Carl T. Rowan
The BVRITH Library
serves as the intellectual hub of
BVRIT Hyderabad Engineering College for Women
, fostering a strong culture of learning, research, and academic excellence among students and faculty. Housed in a modern
four-storey building known as the Ruby Block
, the library is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. It boasts a comprehensive collection of textbooks, reference books, novels, encyclopaedias, magazines, and national and international journals, effectively supporting teaching, learning, and research activities across disciplines.
The library also provides an extensive range of
co-curricular and competitive examination resources
, enabling students to prepare efficiently for various professional and external examinations. By offering access to diverse and updated information sources, the library helps learners gain practical insights into real-world applications and emerging trends.
Actively promoting literary engagement, the library supports various literary events and has established a vibrant literary club,
“PORTIA: The Literary Coven,”
to nurture creativity, critical thinking, and communication skills among students.
The
BVRITH Library
is a proud member of
DELNET
and the
National Digital Library of India (NDLI)
, ensuring seamless access to a vast repository of e-books, journals, and digital learning resources.
All materials are systematically organized using the
Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC)

Library automation
BVRITH library is automated with Engineering College Automation Package (ECAP). The Library catalogue can be accessed through the OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) which provides information of the library collection online. The library OPAC is accessible with the
URL.
BVRITH library also encourages students to access free content from AICTE sponsored Ekumbh website.  The library maintains a Book Bank for SC/ST students which provide additional books offered by the Social Welfare Department, Government of Telangana.
Digital library
BVRITH digital library consists of 10 computers with internet facility. Here, the students can access the subscribed online content, NPTEL video lectures, and can practice online mock tests and so on.
The e- journal packages –
IEEE-ASPP, J-GATE (JET)
are accessible through KNIMBUS mLibrary platform and mlibrary mobile app also.
Remote access – Knimbus e-Library
BVRITH library provides remote access to its subscribed content through
KNIMBUS platform
. This facility can be used by all the staff and students by self-registration with institute mail ID and with self created password. Here the users can access databases, e-Textbooks, e- journals, previous question papers, lecture notes, open access content provided by staff. This knimbus e-library can be accessed through smart phones by downloading mobile app mLibrary from Google Play Store.

e-library
BVRITH e-library provides remote access facility to its subscribed content like IEEE-ASPP, J-GATE (JET) and other uploaded content by the Library. This library is designed by using federated search engine which can search entire subscribed content with help of key words. A user can search the required content with set of key words instead of searching each database separately. The search results can be filtered by using year, publications, type of publication etc. All these facilities can be accessed
here
. The training will be provided at digital library to utilize this e-library.
Plagiarism Check
BVRITH subscribed to iThenticate (a product of Turnitin software) to check the plagiarism of the content of staff and students written for publication. The plagiarism checks are done at
library@bvrithyderabad.edu.in
The final year projects of UG and PG are also checked for plagiarism only when sent through their guide/coordinator.
Rare Books
BVRITH Library has a rare book collection printed from 1919 onwards.
