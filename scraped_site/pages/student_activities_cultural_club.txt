Saarang
“Culture is the intersection of people and life itself. It’s how we deal with life, love, death, birth, disappointment… all of that is expressed in culture”  -Wendell Pierce
Culture is a way of life and an expression of our ethos and core values.The club-
Jhankar
aims at moulding the personality of students and exposes them to a wide range of cultural events throughout the year.
Along with giving a platform for creativity, the Club aims to develop the intellectual, organizational, and social skills of the students. Various skills like leadership, working in a team, sharing success and failure, and accepting responsibility are implicit in the working of the club.
The students develop and organize their own clubs and have the opportunity to demonstrate and develop their leadership and decision-making skills. The club provides a learning experience for the student in organizing, funding, public relations, regulating, and scheduling activities under the direction of the faculty Coordinator Dr V Malathi and Ms A Ramya.
Objectives:
To enhance their skills and to accentuate their understanding and learning graph.
To allow students to express themselves, while enlightening their peers.
To provide a comforting vent for students who are inclined to share their imaginations and talents.
To reach out to the other institutes of the university through various activities and widen theirspectrum.
