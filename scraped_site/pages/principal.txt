Shaping the future of education with passion and purpose.
From Principal's Desk
We constantly strive to provide the best of the faculty members and the State-of-art facilities. The students are encouraged and supported not only to excel in academics, but also to develop a wholesome personality through co-curricular, extra-curricular activities and other extension activities. We create an environment of academic freedom, where bright minds meet, discover and learn. One can experience the Top of the World living and learning experience at BVRITH!
Dr.  K. V. N. Sunitha
PRINCIPAL
Dr. K. V. N. Sunitha
completed her B.Tech. in ECE from ANU, M.Tech. in CS from REC Warangal in 1993 and Ph.D in CSE from JNTU Hyderabad in 2006. She has
30+ years of Teaching Experience
and
15+ years of research experience
. She is working as a Founder Principal, BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women, Hyderabad since August 2012. She received
“
Academic Excellence Award
”
by G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science in 2005,
“
Best computer Science Engineering Teacher award for the year 2007
”
by ISTE in 2008,
“
Distinguished Principal Award
”
by CSI Mumbai in 2017 @ IIT Bombay, Received Deewang Mehtha
“
Women in Education Award
”
in 2017. She was felicitated for
outstanding contribution & achievements in the field of Engineering
in Women Engineers Meet 29
th
Indian Engineering Congress, IEI held at Visveswaraiah Bhavan, Hyderabad on 18
th
Dec 2014. She received “
Engineer of the year 2019 Award
” by IEI, Telangana in 2019. She received “
Acharya Ratna
” –National Award for life time achievement for the year  2019 by Indian servers in association with  AP & Telangana IT Association, in 2019. Received “
Best Engineering College Principal in Telangana State Award
for the year 2019″ by Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) in 2019.  She has guided 20 Ph.D scholars. She authored 5 text books, published more than 185 papers. & 7 patents.

Achievements

ENGINEER OF THE YEAR AWARD

She has been recognized & invited by AICTE as NBA Expert Evaluator. She has authored five  text books,
“Programming in UNIX and Compiler design”
by BS Publications in 2008,
“ Automata Theory ”
by Tata McGrawHill  in 2010,
” Theory of Computation”

“Compiler Construction”

“Formal Languages and Automata Theory”

“Telugu Speech Corrector “
in the book under NOVA Science publishers, USA. She was the editorial board member for prestigious international conferences –ICMEET 2017 and ICICCT 2019, INDIA 2022.
She has guided 20 Ph.D scholars. Her areas of research include Natural Language Processing, Speech Processing, Network & Web Security. She received a research grant from CSI for her R&D project in March 2011 for one year. She also received 38.5 Lakhs of funding for DST TIDE project on “Medicine recognition and alerting system for visually impaired people”,  Rs. 27.32 Lakhs under NSTMIS for the project “Analysis of Career Path of Women Engineers in India”. She has published more than 150 papers in various International &amp; National Journals and conferences. She is a reviewer for many National and International Journals. She is a fellow member of Institute of Engineers, Sr. Member for IEEE; International association CSIT, IAENG and life member of many technical associations like CSI and
ACM.
